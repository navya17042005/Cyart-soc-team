## Root Cause Analysis (5 Whys)

1. Why did the attack succeed?
   → Vulnerable Samba service was exposed.

2. Why was it vulnerable?
   → Outdated software with known exploit.

3. Why was it not patched?
   → Lack of patch management.

4. Why no patching process?
   → Weak security governance.

5. Why weak governance?
   → No structured SOC maturity.

### Root Cause:
Lack of patch management and vulnerability control.
