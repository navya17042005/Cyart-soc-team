## SOC Metrics Calculation

### Incident Timeline
- Incident Time: 12:00 PM  
- Detection Time: 2:00 PM  
- Response Time: 6:00 PM  

### Calculation

- Mean Time to Detect (MTTD) = Detection Time - Incident Time  
  = 2:00 PM - 12:00 PM = 2 hours  

- Mean Time to Respond (MTTR) = Response Time - Detection Time  
  = 6:00 PM - 2:00 PM = 4 hours  

### Result
- MTTD: 2 hours  
- MTTR: 4 hours
